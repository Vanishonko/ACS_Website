About Us

The American College of Sofia is accredited in Bulgaria by the Bulgarian Ministry of Education and Science.
In the United States, the American College of Sofia is accredited by the Middle States Association of Colleges and Schools since 2005.
The American College of Sofia is also certified by the International Baccalaureate Organization as an IB World School since 2004.
